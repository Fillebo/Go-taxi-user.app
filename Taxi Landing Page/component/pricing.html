How it works

Set your location

Use the app to set your pickup location and request a ride. You can also schedule a ride for later.

Choose your ride

You can choose a ride that fits your group size and luggage needs. You’ll also see the estimated wait time for each option.

Get a fare estimate

You can get a fare estimate before you request your ride to help you plan your trip.

Ride with us

Once you’re matched with a driver, you'll see your driver’s name, photo, car information, and real-time location on the map. You’ll also see the driver's ETA to your location.
